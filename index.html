NINE MEN MORRIS

Instructions:
Eliminate opponent pieces by forming a mill(three in a row).
There will be three phases:

  • Phase 1: Users place down their nine pieces
  • Phase 2: Once users have placed all of their pieces, you can now move your pieces to adjacent positions
  • Phase 3: When a user has less than 3 pieces, that user will be able to move their piece to any free location on the board
